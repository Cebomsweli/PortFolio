About Me

My name is Cebolenkosi Msweli. I was born in a small rural area named Shikishela, under the small township Mtubatuba. I attended primary school at Bhokozoza Primary School, then proceeded to Mdungandlovu and later Nkosana High School, where I successfully completed my Matric in the year 2020.

Growing up, I developed a deep passion for technology, which inspired me to pursue a career in Computer Science and IT. This field has not only broadened my understanding of the tech industry but also revealed exciting opportunities and challenges in this ever-evolving domain.

In addition to my love for technology, I enjoy weightlifting, attending church, and engaging in activities that challenge and improve my skills. I also love reading, exploring new technologies, and keeping up with the latest advancements in the tech industry.

My ultimate goal is to continuously grow and adapt as the tech industry evolves, contributing meaningfully to this dynamic field while making a positive impact on the world.
