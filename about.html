Academic Achievements

I have always been passionate about Computer Science and IT, and my academic journey has been a testament to my dedication and hard work in this field. With a strong foundation in mathematics and problem-solving, I have developed expertise in multiple programming languages, including Java, C++, Python, and C#. I have also gained experience in SQL databases, system design, and web development.

I pursued my degree at the University of KwaZulu-Natal from 2021 to 2024, where I refined my technical skills and gained a deeper understanding of software development, data analysis, and system architecture.

During my time at the University of KwaZulu-Natal, I took on the roles of Tutor and Supplemental Instruction (SI) Leader, where I mentored students in Java, Python, and database systems. This experience strengthened my leadership, problem-solving, and communication skills, allowing me to support students in mastering complex concepts.

Throughout my academic career, I have worked on several impactful projects, including:

  • DJ's Booking System: A web-based application designed to simplify event bookings for DJs.
  • Quiz Game: An interactive game that enhances learning through engaging challenges.
  • Career Adviser Chatbot: A web-based chatbot that provides career guidance using AI and API integration.

My goal is to continuously grow and adapt as technology evolves, leveraging my knowledge and skills to make meaningful contributions to the industry. I am always eager to explore new challenges, collaborate on innovative projects, and stay at the forefront of technological advancements.
